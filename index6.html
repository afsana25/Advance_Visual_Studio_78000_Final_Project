R software has used to complete the correlation. The negative value mean they do have inverse relationship, and positive value means they do have positive relationship. The value, 0.60 or more means they have strong relationship.

Conclusion

The analyses show the different idicator relationship in terms of proverty rate, education score, and per capita of United states. The relationship between education score and per capita of each state is highly positive.
